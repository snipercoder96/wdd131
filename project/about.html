About HyperMarkup Mentor

HyperMarkup Mentor is a learning webpage designed to help beginner developers master the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through practical tips, clear examples, and interactive exercises, this site makes web development fun and exciting.

Created by Kevin Mbemba Kiyindou, HyperMarkup Mentor reflects my journey as a developer. I built this project to share what I’ve learned, highlight common pitfalls and gotchas, and provide resources that make coding smoother for beginners and intermediate learners alike.

Whether you’re just starting out or refining your skills, HyperMarkup Mentor is here to guide you step by step, with a focus on accessibility, clean design, and maintainable code.

Our Mission

Helping developers learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through practical tips and interactive examples.
