Developer Tips:

Practical advice to make your coding smoother and more efficient.
Here are the following tips:

Use Semantic HTML

Always choose the right elements for the job — it improves accessibility and SEO (Search Engine Optimization).

Flexbox vs Grid

Flexbox is best for one-dimensional layouts, while Grid shines in two-dimensional designs.

Debugging with DevTools

Inspect elements, check CSS rules, and use the console to track down issues quickly.

JavaScript Functions

Ensure you break down particular functions to doing one job, that way, its easier to break down your work in easy steps.

Start with HTML basics

Ensure master the basic concepts such as <html>, <head>, <title>, <body>, and <p>.

Dealing with burnout

As a Developer, you need rest, if you feel tired, ensure that you take a break and look outside. A saying goes by: "Sometimes you have to take a step back to move forward" - Erika Taylor

Practice, Practice, Practice

Heard a phrase practice makes perfect, yes, sharpen your skills by practicing and also making mistakes.

Create projects

Create even small projects, they are a booster confidence, they are going to help you understand the basics.
